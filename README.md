# 🚗 AutoMarket360 -- Automotive Market Intelligence Dashboard

## 🔎 Overview

**AutoMarket360** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to
analyze automotive market data and uncover key insights related to
pricing, mileage, fuel types, transmission types, and vehicle
conditions.

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of market trends, helping
stakeholders understand car listings, pricing behavior, and distribution
patterns across multiple dimensions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

-   Analyze listed car market trends\
-   Monitor average price fluctuations over time\
-   Understand fuel type and transmission distribution\
-   Evaluate vehicle condition breakdown\
-   Identify top car manufacturers in the market

## 📈 Dashboard Insights & Features

### 1️⃣ Manufacturer Analysis

-   Top Car Makers by Listing Count\
-   Brand-wise market presence comparison

### 2️⃣ Pricing Trends

-   Average Price by Year\
-   Historical price fluctuation analysis\
-   Identification of price growth trends

### 3️⃣ Market Distribution

-   Count of Cars by Fuel Type (Diesel, Electric, Hybrid, Gasoline,
    Petrol)\
-   Transmission Type Distribution (Automatic vs Manual)\
-   Vehicle Condition Breakdown (New, Like New, Used)

### 4️⃣ Vehicle Attributes Analysis

-   Count of Car Models by Color\
-   Mileage distribution\
-   Condition-based segmentation

### 5️⃣ Interactive Filters

-   Transmission Filter\
-   Condition Filter\
-   Fuel Type Filter\
-   Dynamic slicers for interactive exploration

## 🧠 Key Insights Derived

-   Diesel and Electric vehicles show strong representation in
    listings.\
-   Automatic and Manual transmissions have nearly equal market
    presence.\
-   Used cars dominate overall listings compared to new vehicles.\
-   Average car price shows a significant upward trend in recent years.\
-   Premium brands like Rolls-Royce and McLaren lead in high-value
    listings.
